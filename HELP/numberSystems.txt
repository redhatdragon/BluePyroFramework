bin(binary)
base 2:		01
NOTE: mostly used to explain bit related operations on the cpu

oct(octal)
base 8:		01234567

dec(decimal)
base 10:	0123456789
NOTE: the one mose people usually use

hex(hexidecimal)
base 16:	0123456789abcdef
NOTE: most common way to read a byte(s)

Examples to reach the number 12:
bin:	1100
oct:	14
dec:	12
hex:	c

Examples to reach the number 16:
bin:	1111
oct:	20
dec:	16
hex:	f
